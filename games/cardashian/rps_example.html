How To

Cardashiane is a Javascript take on rock, paper, scissors.

  1. Select a Cardashiane sister.
  2. Wait a few seconds.
  3. The browser will randomly make a selection to counter.
  4. The winning play is displayed
  5. Make another selection to play again.
