Who Wee Are

Wee Rescue is an all-volunteer organization dedicated to rescuing Lhasa Apsos, Shih Tzus, and Pekingese from Central Texas shelters. Our mission is to place these homeless dogs into safe, loving, permanent homes in the Greater Austin Area.

We are a 501(c)(3) organization. All donations are tax deductible. Visit our donation page to make a contribution.
